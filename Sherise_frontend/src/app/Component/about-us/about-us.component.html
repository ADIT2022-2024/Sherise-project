Our Mission

Raising Awareness About Menstruation in Rural Areas

Our mission is to break the silence and dismantle the stigma surrounding menstruation in rural areas through comprehensive education and community engagement. We aim to empower individuals, particularly young girls and women, with accurate information about menstrual health, hygiene practices, and the importance of menstrual equity. By providing accessible educational programs, we strive to foster an environment where menstruation is understood as a natural and healthy part of life, free from shame and misconceptions.

Our Vision

We envision a future where all individuals in rural areas have the knowledge and resources to manage their menstruation with dignity and confidence. By raising awareness and providing the necessary support, we aim to reduce absenteeism in schools, improve overall health, and promote gender equality. Through our dedicated efforts, we strive to create a world where menstruation is no longer a barrier to education, health, or social participation.
