kagaj ke Pad

Exploring the innovative solution of handmade sanitary pads, "Kagaj Ke Pad" aims to address menstrual health challenges in rural areas. Our mission is to empower women with sustainable menstrual products and promote hygiene through education and community support.

Our approach involves training local women to produce affordable, eco-friendly sanitary pads from easily accessible materials. By fostering community engagement and creating educational programs, we aim to enhance awareness about menstrual health and hygiene, ensuring that women have access to safe and sustainable menstrual products.

Project Components

Fostering awareness and sustainable menstrual well-being through education and support in rural Greater Noida.

Menstrual Health Support
