Pad Bank

Unveiling the hidden challenges surrounding menstruation, shedding light on disparities, and advocating for change through Pad Bank. Our mission is to break the silence and dismantle the stigma surrounding menstruation through the free distribution of sanitary pads and comprehensive education workshops.

Old papers collected as donations from households help procure sanitary pads. Our approach includes community mobilization, the creation of informative and engaging IEC (Information, Education, and Communication) materials, and comprehensive education sessions. These initiatives are designed to create sustainable changes in attitudes and practices regarding menstrual health.

Project Components

Fostering awareness and sustainable menstrual well-being through education and support in rural Greater Noida.

Community Engagement Events

Eco-friendly Pads Manufacturing

Awareness Campaigns

Project at a Glance

Reaching out across seven villages in Greater Noida, aiming to uplift countless lives with crucial health education and resources.
