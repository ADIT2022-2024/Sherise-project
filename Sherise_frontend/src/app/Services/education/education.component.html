Educational Workshops Empowering Girls with Knowledge and Support

Our educational workshops are designed to empower girls by providing them with essential knowledge and support on crucial topics such as menstrual health, hygiene, and overall well-being. These workshops aim to create a safe and supportive environment where girls can learn, ask questions, and build confidence.

Topics Covered

  • Understanding the Menstrual Cycle
  • Menstrual Hygiene Practices
  • Nutrition and Menstrual Health
  • Emotional and Psychological Support

Offline places where our workshop Takes place

  • Schools and Colleges: Conducting workshops in educational institutions can reach a younger audience and provide crucial information early on.
  • Health Clinics and Hospitals: These settings are ideal as they already focus on health education and can provide resources and support.
  • Local NGOs and Women's Organizations: Partnering with local non-profits and organizations focused on women's health and education can provide access to their networks and resources.
  • Community Halls and Recreation Centers: These venues are designed for community gatherings and may have facilities suitable for workshops.
